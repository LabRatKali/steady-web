    Rules of the race

    How competitions work

    Four tracks. One opt-in profile. Points follow how you use your phone — not how loud you are online.

    • Cleanest day

      Overall score from Focus, Work, and Learn minutes, streak strength, and resisted Fun opens — minus Fun time.

    • Focus race

      Who held Focus longest. Deep sessions and Focus badges pull you ahead of the pack.

    • Essentials only

      Survive on essentials apps. Low Fun, high discipline — Essentials Day and Monk Mode shine here.

    • One-day sprint

      A single clean day, ranked hot. Reset energy for people who want a sharp, short race.

    What you earn

    Rewards

    League points feed a point shop in the app. Parents can grant Full, Half, or Token rewards when kids hit goals.

    • Point shop

      Spend reward points on unlocks and treats you set — earned from clean days and competition ranks.

    • Parent grants

      Full, Half, or Token — parents approve the size when a goal lands.

    • Badges

      Streaks, Focus depth, and essentials days mint badges that show on your public row.

    Your call

    Privacy

    League is opt-in. Private by default. When you go public, Steady shares only aggregated stats you choose — score, streak, clean minutes, Fun penalty, badges. No messages, no location, no app list, no stalking.

    Manage visibility in the Steady app: ⋯ → Steady League.
