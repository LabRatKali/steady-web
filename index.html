FAQ

Straight answers.

Is Steady only for parents?

No. Use This phone is for me for adult self-focus — schedules, Focus, Sleep, optional accountability friend. Parenting roles are there when you need them.

What’s “Steady as Home”?

Steady becomes your Home screen. Pressing Home returns to Steady; you see a grid of allowed apps. You’re not fighting a separate launcher.

How do I pause or uninstall for testing?

Apply Essentials (or open Settings), unlock with your codes, tap Pause Steady. Locks stop. Then you can deactivate Device Admin and uninstall if you want.

Do I need a computer?

No for normal use. Admin + Accessibility + usage access are on-phone. A computer is only for optional Device Owner (uninstall lock).

Is it free?

Yes. Download the APK here. No subscription wall on current builds.

Which languages?

English, Hebrew, Yiddish, Spanish, Russian — RTL for Hebrew and Yiddish.
